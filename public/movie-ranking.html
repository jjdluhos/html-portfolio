Jarret's Top Movies

Below are my top 3 movies of all time

Gladiator

This is an amazing action/drama movie. The movie stars Russel Crowe and has really intense action scenes with a lot of blood and gore.

Split

This is my favorite scary/horror/suspense movie of all time. This movie is about a man with multiple split personalities and explores the idea of him being superhuman.

V for Vendetta

This is a very intellectually stimulating movie. The movie explores the complexity of power and fear used by governments to control thier populace.
